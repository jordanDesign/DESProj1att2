ABOUT US

Bay Area Art Collective is a community of artists who vend and share their artwork in person and over social media. Central to the bay area these artists work hard to receive recognition and maintain business as fine artists. All artists on this site are looking for vendor locations, storefronts, pop-ups, and any other event where you may desire their artwork! Each week we update feature artists and share events around the area. We are non-profit and appreciate donations to keep us going. All funds go back to artists and the curation of their spaces.
